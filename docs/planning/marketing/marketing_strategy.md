# 🎯 Marketing Strategy: Stängelispass

Stängelispass is not just a beer tracker; it's the digital companion for legendary nights. Our marketing focuses on **Social Proof**, **Competition**, and **Premium Simplicity**.

## 💎 Core Value Proposition
"Never lose count of the round again. Honor the legends, track the truth."

---

## 🚀 Rollout Phases

### Phase 1: The Inner Circle (Days 1-7)
- **Target**: Local pub regulars and close friend groups.
- **Action**: Direct seeding. One person starts an "Event Pass" and invites the squad.
- **Goal**: Establish the "Wall of Fame" with real data.

### Phase 2: Social Proof Engine (Days 8-21)
- **Viral Hook**: Use the **MVP Recap Modal** stickers. 
- **Platform**: Instagram/WhatsApp Stories.
- **Strategy**: Encourage users to share the "Brewmaster of the Night" recap with the hashtag #Stängelispass.

### Phase 3: The Society Scale (Day 22+)
- **Target**: Student associations, societies, and social clubs.
- **Action**: Offer a "Society Pass" (Bulk event rounds).
- **Goal**: Become the official logging tool for organized events.

## ⚔️ Competitor Landscape

Stängelispass carves a unique niche as a "Social Utility" — avoiding the complexity of rating apps and the clinical nature of health trackers.

| Feature | **Untappd** | **My Bar / Inventory Apps** | **DrinkControl / Sunnyside** | **Stängelispass** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Goal** | Discovery & Ratings | Cocktail Recipes & Inventory | Health & Moderation | **Social Frictionless Logging** |
| **Vibe** | "Beer Nerd" / Reviewer | "Mixologist" / Stock Manager | "Doctor" / Clinical | **"The Pub Regular"** |
| **Key Mechanic** | 5-Star Ratings & Badges | Ingredient Matching | Calorie/Unit Counting | **Rounds, Costs, & "Who Pays?"** |
| **Offline Mode** | Poor (Requires DB check) | Good (Static Data) | Good (Simple Log) | **Excellent (Local First)** |
| **Complexity** | High (Heavy UI) | Medium | Medium | **Low (One-Thumb Use)** |

### Key Differentiators
1.  **Speed**: We are faster than Untappd. No searching for "Generic Lager 2024"—just tap +1.
2.  **Money**: We track the *cost* of the night, not just the liquid.
3.  **Gamification**: We gamify the *group dynamic* (Who pays?), not just individual consumption.

## 🧭 SWOT Analysis

### 💪 Strengths (Internal)
-   **Micro-Niche Utility**: Solves the specific problem of "Coaster counting" better than any notepad app.
-   **Visceral UX**: Heavy Haptics create a satisfying "physical" interaction that digital counters lack.
-   **Local-First Speed**: Zero latency logging is critical in poor-signal bars/festivals.
-   **Swiss Branding**: "Stängelispass" implies precision and cultural authenticity to the target market.

### ⚠️ Weaknesses (Internal)
-   **No Cloud Sync (MVP)**: Losing a phone means losing history (mitigated by CSV export).
-   **Limited Discovery**: Users cannot "find" new beers or venues (intentional, but a feature gap).
-   **Platform Lock**: iOS only (initially).

### 🌍 Opportunities (External)
-   **Events & Festivals**: Partnering with local fests (e.g., Zürichfest) for an "Official Tally App".
-   **Merchandise**: The "Stängelispass" aesthetic translates well to physical coasters and openers.
-   **B2B / Groups**: "Society Pass" for student fraternities to manage large bar tabs.

### 🌪️ Threats (External)
-   **"Good Enough" Alternatives**: Apple Notes or a physical coaster remain the biggest friction to overcome.
-   **Feature Creep**: Adding too many "Untappd-like" features could dilute the simplicity proposition.
-   **Competitor Pivots**: If Untappd adds a robust "Table/Round" mode, they could encroach on our niche.
