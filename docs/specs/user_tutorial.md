# 🍺 Stängelispass: The Ultimate Guide

Welcome to the future of beer tracking! Here is how you use Stängelispass to dominate your next night out.

## 1. Start a Round
Before tracking begins, someone in your squad must start a round. This activates the leaderboard for the next 24 hours.

![Start a Round](file:///Users/ppf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/519f81e7-7e71-4de6-8324-99e03672d3ac/tutorial_step_1_start_round_1770554179277.png)

- **Cost**: Only $0.99 per round (Squad discount!).
- **Hook**: Your first round is always on us! 🍻

## 2. Log Your Drinks
Once the round is active, an Admin can log drinks for anyone in the squad.

![Add Beer](file:///Users/ppf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/519f81e7-7e71-4de6-8324-99e03672d3ac/tutorial_step_2_add_beer_1770554194241.png)

1. Go to the **Add** tab.
2. Select the member who just finished a drink.
3. Tap **Add 1 Beer!** and feel that satisfying success haptic. ⚡️

## 3. Claim Your Glory (MVP Recap)
When the night is over, hit "End Round" on the Home screen to trigger the Hall of Fame.

![MVP Win](file:///Users/ppf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/519f81e7-7e71-4de6-8324-99e03672d3ac/tutorial_step_3_mvp_win_1770554213573.png)

- **MVP Card**: The winner gets a stylized badge.
- **Viral Sharing**: Tap the "Share" button to post your squad's dominance to Instagram or WhatsApp instantly. 📸

---

### Pro Tips:
- **Admin Lock**: Only Admins can add beers to prevent "ghost logging".
- **History**: Check the **History** tab to settle any morning-after debates about who actually won.
